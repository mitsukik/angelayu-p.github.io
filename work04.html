Industrial IoT System

BACKGROUND AND OBJECTIVES

This client is an industrial company is establishing a Production Process Information System with the primary objective of digitizing the manufacturing process. The key focus is on integrating data from the current internal Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system to achieve heterogeneous system integration. This integration aims to gain insights into production progress and associated production costs.

TEAM:

Project Manager/System Analyst, UI/UX Front-end Designer, Back-end developer, QA Engineer

ROLE:

UI/UX Front-end Designer, Basic frontend web development

TIMELINE:

2023 APR - 2023 AUG about 4 months

Link: Demo SVG Floor Plan

TO PROBLEM & SOLUTION

Challenge

The challenge at hand was to digitize and interconnect the manufacturing process, addressing the needs of operators with diverse nationalities and using different devices. The objective was to develop a UI/UX design that seamlessly integrates into a complex system, ensuring accessibility and efficiency for a globally diverse user base.


PROBLEM & SOLUTION

  1. Multifaceted Accessibility:
    • Problem: Operators from different nationalities may encounter language barriers and varying levels of technological familiarity.
    • Solution: Focus on icon and no text, designed an intuitive multilingual interface with clear icons and controls to provide a consistent user experience, irrespective of language differences.
  2. Device Agnosticism:
    • Problem: Operators using a variety of devices may face inconsistencies in the user interface.
    • Solution: Implemented a responsive design strategy, adapting the interface seamlessly to different devices. Ensured touch-friendly controls for those using devices on the production floor.
  3. Customizable:
    • Problem: Navigating the complexities of manufacturing operations and addressing the diverse preferences of operators posed a significant challenge.
    • Solution: Designed a simplified navigation structure with easily recognizable icons, reducing the learning curve for operators dealing with intricate manufacturing processes. Also, introduced customizable dashboards, allowing operators to tailor interfaces to their distinct preferences and responsibilities. This feature not only accommodated individual needs but also significantly enhanced overall user efficiency.
