Fashion APP & CMS

WORK:
Shopping APP, Content Management System

CLIENT:
LUDOVIK

TEAM:
Project Manager/System Analyst, UI/UX Front-end Designer, Back-end developer, QA Engineer

ROLE:
UI/UX Front-end Designer, Basic frontend web development

USER:
Public

TIMELINE（my part）:
2020 FEB - 2020 MAR

BACKGROUND:
The client wants to develop a fashion e-commerce app with a design similar to Apple's minimalistic style. The goal was to enable users to easily purchase clothing and share their unboxing photos. Additionally, a management system was needed for content and functionality management. Due to the tight timeline, the entire project only had a month for the whole development.

Link: Web APP Demo Link

Project Progress:

  1. Requirement Confirmation:
    Once the basic requirements were confirmed with the client, we swiftly entered the design phase.
  2. UI/UX Design Reference:
    To offer an optimal design solution, we quickly sought references from similar products for UI/UX inspiration. This allowed us to better understand market trends and deliver a design more aligned with user expectations.
  3. Product Differentiation and Web App Building:
    Based on our experience, we analyze the features of the e-commerce app, classify the different products of the shop, and start building the home page, single product pages, member's personal pages, and shopping process, member registration process.
  4. Rapid Prototype Development:
    Concurrently with the design, we developed a prototype to quickly showcase the initial design concepts. The prototype served for communication between the project manager (PM) and the client, facilitating testing of the workflow and confirming features.
  5. Additional Page and Management System Design:
    While awaiting client confirmation, we incorporated supplementary pages, such as terms of service and a company introduction. Concurrently, we initiated the development of the management system's features, integrating the functionality analysis of the Web APP into the requirements of the management system.

Design Style:

The main color scheme for the app consists of black and white, reflecting Apple's minimalist aesthetic. The management system color scheme was customized based on the editor's preferences for enhanced usability.

Results:

This project successfully delivered a fashion e-commerce app and its corresponding management system within the client's limited timeframe. Our design not only prioritized user experience but also considered the client's brand style and management requirements. Through a rapid and organized development process, we ensured the efficient delivery of the product.
